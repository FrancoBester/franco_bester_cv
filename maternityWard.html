Maternity Ward

We wholeheartedly embrace mother and baby friendly principles, prioritizing the well-being of both.

New parents are warmly encouraged to bring their birth plan, and we ensure routine skin-to-skin care following birth, irrespective of the delivery method.

Additionally, our facility promotes rooming in, which allows babies to stay with their mothers.

This practice fosters bonding and enhances the mother-child relationship.

Moreover, while we actively promote breastfeeding, we also fully support and respect the mother's choice in selecting the most suitable feeding method for her baby.
