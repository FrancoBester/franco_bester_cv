Valuables

We strongly recommend that you leave all your valuables at home. Cormed Clinic does not take any resposibility for the loss of valuables.

You will only need essentials such as toiletries and sleepwear in your room.

Meals

Our in-house Food Service Departemnt will take care of all of your dietary needs during your stay.

You will receive 3 meals daily.

What To Bring

On the day of admission, make sure you have the following with you:
